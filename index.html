FAQs

What if I have no desserts in my list?

We include tasteful default dessert suggestions automatically, like fruit salad or yogurt with honey. You can still add your own at any time.

Can I print each week?

Yes. The app includes a print layout that breaks by week and fits nicely on paper with high contrast.

Does it save my foods?

Your foods and plans are saved in your browser using localStorage so your work persists across reloads.
